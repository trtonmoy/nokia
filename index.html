How to Buy

  • Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app.

  • Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $NOK. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy SOL from an exchange or cross chain swap and send it to your wallet.

  • Go to Raydium, Jupiter..

Connect to Raydium or Jupiter. Go raydium.io or jup.ag in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $NOK token address into Raydium or Jupiter and confirm the swap. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
